Ice Water

This timeless classic never fails to hydrate!

Ingredients

  • One glass - It doesn't have to be clear, but it helps.
  • Water - We prefer artisanal glacier runoff, but store-bought is fine.
  • Ice - Otherwise it would just be a glass of water.
  • Lemon Slices - (Optional).

Steps

  1. Grasp the glass firmly in your dominant hand.
  2. Using your off-hand, place ice in the glass.
  3. Position the glass directly in the path of the mouth of the water distribution device.
  4. Tilt the water distribution device until water begins to flow into the glass.
  5. When the glass is full, restore the water distribution device to its upright and locked position.
  6. Garnish with lemon slices if desired.
